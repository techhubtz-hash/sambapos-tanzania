What SambaPOS does

Everything your restaurant needs,
in one system.

Built for restaurants, bars, cafés and hotels. Runs on any Windows PC or tablet you already own.

🖨️

Kitchen order printing

Orders sent directly to the kitchen printer the moment they're placed — no running, no shouting, no mistakes.

🪑

Table management

Visual floor plan, live table status, split bills, table transfers, and multiple waiters on one floor.

Multiple payment types
